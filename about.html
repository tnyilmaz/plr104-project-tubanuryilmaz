About the Author

Born and raised in the historical heart of Safranbolu, Tuba Nur Yılmaz is an award-winning author renowned for her captivating books exploring the intersection of urban wildlife and cultural heritage.

Working from her restored 300-year-old Ottoman mansion, Tuba weaves tales that give a voice to the silent, furry guardians of the ancient wooden houses.
